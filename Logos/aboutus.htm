Perpetual Motion Sports & Entertainment Inc.

Perpetual Motion Sports & Entertainment Inc. specializes in active and social events in Guelph, Ontario.  We offer co-ed sports leagues, various tournaments & social outings.  Our sports leagues are a great way to get exercise, meet up with friends or make new ones.  All our games are co-ed and self officiated.  At the end of each season, trophies and prizes are awarded to the top team and the team who played with the most spirit as voted by the other teams in the league.  So, if you are looking for some excitement check out our website for something that interests you.
