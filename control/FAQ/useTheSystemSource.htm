Changing A Season (Home)
When changing a season make sure the dates are correct for 'season_registration_open' and 'season_registration_stop_showing'. A season is defined as 'current' if 'season_available_score_reporter' = 1 and season_available_registration = 1 means that season's leagues will show up for registration. Make sure the season you're going into is always in the database. The team registration program will check to see which seasons should be available for what, and set them accordingly. Along with making sure seasons are correct, you should also make sure that all the leagues for the next season are created and available to register for.
Creating a League (Home)
To create a league, login to the control panel then choose 'Create a League' from the leagues dropdown. From there, choose the season, and sport you want associated with the league. Choosing a sport will default alot of the variables but here are small definitions for each:

League Name: The name that will show up for EVERYTHING involving that league
Registration Fee: Fee for teams
Max Points: Max points that will show up in the score reporter
Individual Fee: Fee for Individuals
Num of Matches: Number of different teams one team will play a night (ie beach volleyball = 2, A2 & B7 = 1)
Num of Games: Number of games played against a singl team (Vball = 2, rest = 1)
Has Ties: If tie should show up in the score reporter and in standings
Has Practice: If a league has practice matches, makes it so practice is an option for that league in the score reporter
Week to Start at: What week the league is currently in, if you're creating a new league set this to 1
Playoffs Start Week: What week playoffs start at, this sets when standings won't be updated and error checking pretty much gets turned off
Allow Individuals: Does nothing... eventually will determine which leagues show up for individual registration
Teams before Waiting List: Does nothing.. kindve, does nothing if set to 0, at one point will inform people that the league is full and they're being put on the waiting list
Max Teams: Does nothing if set to 0, otherwise will tell people they can't register for the league if too many teams are already in it
Ask for Scores: Should scores be asked for in the score repoter?
Sort by Percentage: Should this league be sorted by win % in standings
Days Spirit will be hidden: Number of days after their matches are played that the spirits will be hidden
Spirit Hours: What hour should spirit be hidden on the day games are played and what hour should they be reshown a certain amount of days later
Picture Link: The link that points to that any team in that leagues team picture
Editing a League (Home)
Editing a league is super easy, just go to the control panel and choose 'Update a League' in the leagues dropdown. From here just choose what season, sport, and league you want to edit and it will bring you to the edit page. Once you're dont editing all the league values just click submit and you're done!
Editing team/player Registrations (Home)
When people register as individuals/teams they get put in the database and on the control panel for editing. This means you can change player names/ emails/ genders/ notes status, along with a teams league/ name/ convenor status/ player info/etc. In order to change a team/ players data there are 3 all equally valid methods:
  • - Go to 'edit a league' from the leagues drop down, and enter the information for the team/player you want to edit - On this page you can edit a teams number in the league, or their convenor status - You can change specific team info from here by clicking on the team you want to edit
    - You will be brought to the teams editor page, now you can change the team data you wish - If you want to edit a player on this team, click on that specific player
    - Now you'll be brough to the players editing menu, where you can make all the changes you'd like then click update a player
  • - If you need to edit a team name, team league, or team player info you can go to 'edit a team' from the teams drop down. - Now enter the information for the team you want, and you can change team level information
    - If you want to edit player information on that team, click on a link for a player - This brings you to the players editor page, from here you can make all the changes you'd like then click update a player
  • - If you want to only edit a certain players data, go to 'edit a player' from the players drop down - Enter in the data for that player and you can make all the changes you'd like then click update a player to finish
Adding free agents to teams (Home)
If the team exists:
- Go to 'edit a player' from the players manu, then punch in the sports and league your free agent has signed up for. Next, choose that players 'new team' from the drop down, and press 'update.'
If the new team doesn't exist:
Go to 'Add a team' from the teams drop down on the control panel. Choose the sport/season/league you want to add the team to, input the name and press the 'Submit Team' button on the bottom of the form. Now, go to 'Edit a Team' from the teams drop down, and re-enter the top data. To add free agents to the team, click the checkboxes of all the players you want to go to the team, then press 'Add to team' on the bottom right corner of the screen. Eventually you will be able to enter a teams captain from this page but for now, to enter the captain go to 'Edit teams with no captain,' find the team you want to edit, input the captain data, and press 'Submit captains'
Loading Schedules into the Database (Home)
Before loading a schedule into the database, make sure all the team numbers in the league match up with the team numbers on your schedule. To do this, open 'Edit registrations by League' in the leagues drop down, and use the number drop downs in the middle of the screen to set the numbers you desire. Note that by default the auto changer is set, to turn it off toggle the checkbox below the numbers drop downs. Once you have all the numbers set the way they are on the schedule, click 'update Nums' on the bottom of the screen and you're done!

As for loading the schedules, it's an easy job, with a few known bugs. Go to 'Load a schedule into the database' from the leagues dropdown. Enter the sport, season, and league then get the URL for the schedule page that was put online. On chrome, getting the URL entails going to the schedule page, right clicking the page, and selecting 'view frame source.' On internet explorer, the best way i found was right clicking the page and selecting 'properties.' For both cases, you want the full URL from the 'www.' to the '.htm'. Input this URL into the text box and press submit. To make sure it worked properly, check to see if there's an even amount of games for each game day.
KNOWN BUGS:
Practice games must be against some permutation of 'practice' with a field name for the field (anything with 'open', or 'field' works)
If the venue is mis-spelled or not in the database, the games for that venue will not be put in the database. This will result in the number of games for a day to be different than the rest. To fix this add the missing venue to the venues database.
Loading Venues into the Database (Home)
To add a venue to the database, simply open the 'venues_dbtable' in navicat. Add a venue into the bottom of the table, and fill in the fields. venue_name is for whatever will be put into a schedule cell, venue_short_name is a simpler version used mostly for error checking, venue_link is a link to the map page for that venue.
During A Season (Home)
During seasons the system pretty much takes care of itself. Some troubles might come up in the score reporter but during the beta season I only had 2 issues, both of which got fixed. Just make sure the standings make sense, and every team has a score submission before their next week and stuff will move smooth.
Editing score reports (Home)
Using shear genius, I have taken editing score reporting from 3 steps to 1. Simply open 'Fix standings' in the leagues menu, input your sport, league, and week, and change whatever is wrong to whatever you believe is right. This will update the standings, team pages in the control panel, and whatever else may reference it.
Creating Phantom Spirits (Home)
Sometimes it is benificial to create spirit scores for teams (eg so that at the beginning of the season N/A won't show up for a team's spirit). Simply open 'Create Spirit Scores' from the teams drop down, and enter the top info as usual. Once the team is entered you'll see a drop down under the teams drop down for choosing the spirit you want to give the current team, and a button for submitting. All 'phantom' spirit scores you create are kept in the holding box at the bottom of the form, and you can delete them any time by checking their checkbox and pressing 'Delete.'
Splitting Leagues (Home)
In order to split a league into two new leagues follow these steps:
  1. Create both the new leagues
  2. Go to 'Split a League' in the leagues drop down
  3. Enter in all the sport and league information, then all the teams should show up from the 'League to be split' in order of their standings.
  4. The 1, 2 dropdowns are all for which new league will the team go to, edit any if necessary then press 'Setup' to complete the league splitting
NOTE: When entering the two new league's schedule data you will run into problems with which week each new date is associated with. In order to fix this open 'Load a schedule into the database' from the leagues menu, and punch in the data for your new league. When you enter in the new schedule data you will see all the weeks you submitted. You will also notice they are ordered 1, 2, 3, etc. which is probably wrong. What you want is to set those week numbers to whatever week they will be in the score reporter, then the score reporter and standings pages can keep running smooth.
Turning on the Cancel Option (Home)
Open 'Turn on Cancel' in the leagues dropdown. This will bring up all the current leagues in the score reporter. Click on which leagues you want the cancel option to be turned on for then click 'Turn on Cancel' on the bottom of the form. Done! :)
Team Pictures (Home)
For team pictures, you must first make sure the folder that you put the pictures in is the same for the leagues 'league_pic_link.' By default this is set to the same system as what was used in 2012. To check this picture link, go to 'edit a league' in the leagues drop down of the control panel, and punch in the league you're looking for. At the bottom of the screen you should see the picture link for that league, you can also edit it from here. To find out a pictures name open 'Team Picture ID's' from the teams dropdown and name each picture the teamID given with extension '.jpg' or '.JPG'. If there are two teams with the same picture ID you can change one of them in their picture text box and press 'Update Teams' on the bottom of the form. Put the pictures in the required folders and the league picture pages should figure it out from there. Of course for now there is still the tedious job of making the new league link on the pictures main page... but you should know how to do that.
Making sure all Teams have Captains (Home)
This is only important for the emailing captains function on the control panel, and the late email that gets sent to captains. To check what teams have no team captain go to 'Edit teams with no Captain' in the teams drop down. As of August 7th this only gets teams that are available in the score reporter however i will fix it so it works for teams in registration too. To use this program, enter the name / email/ other info for any teams then press Submit. If you have submitted anything for the fields 'first name', 'last name', and a valid email then the team will be granted a new captain.
Closing Standings For a Week (Home)
Before a week can be completed and the score reporter/ standings can move on every team in that league must report scores. This can be done either with the score reporter on the public website, or the fix standings section of the control panel. With the score reporter, simply punch in the league and team that needs to submit their scores and press 'Submit', warning this does get error checked. With the fix standings program punch in the sport, league, and week then edit any teams data and press submit. This does not get error checked and will create/ update any score submissions accordinly.
End of Season Routines (Home)
There is almost nothing that needs to be done at the end of a season. Most of the stuff that needs to be done is for starting a new season. Never the less you will have to close out standings for good! Also, the score reporter will know it should be in playoff mode when the week it's in is the same or greater than the playoff start week.
Closing out Standings for Good (Home)
This program makes it so you will never have to make a hookup another html page for the final standings. Go to the 'Close out a League' program of the leagues drop down, and input the sport and league you want to close standings for. The leagues for this are the ones available in the score reporter. When a league is selected, they will be sorted by their final position and spirits by their final spirits. If you want to order the league any differently you can using the drop downs, and once you're done press 'Close Standings' to have the new final standings page show up for that league.

Tournaments (Home)
Key things to know about tournaments is that all the tournaments available for use are in the 'tournament' database. All tournament teams and players are associated with one of these tournaments. Also good to know is a tournament is defined by 'tournament_num_running,' this attribute separates ones years tournament from another. To create a new tournament all together just add a row to the 'tournaments' database and fill in the columns as necessary.
Editing a Tournament Setup (Home)
When I say Editing a tournament setup I mean stuff like, is registration for teams or players? Are there cards? What are the prices, when is it? etc. To edit anything about the tournament go to 'edit a tournament' from the tournaments drop down. Punch in which tournament you want and change any of the data as you see fit. I would recomment not touching 'num running' unless you're finishing one tournament and setting up next years. The rest is all very interactive and filled to the brim with javascript. The 'Extra Field' column is basically just for cities in Stall Fall currently, but if you want to add any new thing to a tournament there's a good spot. It's good to note that I believe the extra field option only works with teams right now, sorry :). It should also be good to note that is cards and is players are two different things. There are currently no setups of tournaments that only take players without the card system but just in case one is made the functionality is there.

Changings isTeams to isPlayers or isCards affects what shows up for the registration pages, and the teams entered pages. Both those pages are coded to work with all three so total control is given to the create tournaments page.
Editing Tournament Leagues (Home)
Editing a tournaments leagues means basically editing what teams are entered into the tournament and editing a little data about them. To edit this information go to 'Edit a League' in the tournaments dropdown. Punch in your tournament and you're off to the races. From here you can change any data anywhere, even add in new data, and when you click 'Submit Teams' on the bottom of the page, all information on the page is submitted. Make sure there are no doubles of numbers in a league as this results in one of the teams not showing up anymore. It doesn't mean they're deleted from the database, just change the numbers back and they'll show up again. To delete teams from here, check them off on the right of the screen then press 'Delete Checked Teams' on the bottom of the page. The 'Printable Page' button merely shows all the teams in the tournament on a new page in a more printer friendly format. The 'Email League' links on the page are for emailing all the captains in each league. These work if GMail is the default mailto program, however outlook seems to like being the default so if it is, these links don't work very well.
Editing Tournament Teams (Home)
Editing tournament teams means basically editing what players are on a team, changing the team name, or even changing the team's league. To edit a team, find the team you want in 'Edit a League' and click the 'edt' button beside their team name. This will bring you to a new screen where you can edit everything I stated before. To change a team name enter in the new name beside the change team name button and press 'Change Team Name.' Changing a league is alot the same, just click the new league and press 'Change League.' Before either of these buttons will submit they require reassurance by means of an 'are you sure' popup window. By default the first player in the database hooked up to the team is considered the team captain. Therefore, to change the team captain add a new player by filling in all the information on the bottom of the form and press 'Create Player.' After this, delete the first player that was considered the captain before. Now the new player will show up on the leagues page with their email that you submitted earlier.
Editing Tournament Players/Cards (Home)
This page is here almost exclusively so that you don't need to go to the database for ANYTHING. If you get an email saying a player wants their card changed or someone changes their last name and is adament that their new one is what the website says, this is what to do:
- If they want their card changed, find their name on the 'edit a league' page, find their name, change their card, and press 'Submit Data'
- If they want their name changed either find their team or name on the edit league page. If theyre on a team, press 'edt' beside their team name, then on the team page you get brought to press their name. If theyre not on a team, press their name.
- From the edit players page you should be on, change their last name or email or whatever and press 'update player.'
Finishing a Tournament (Home)
This is important. When you finish a tournament go to 'Edit a Tournament' from the tournaments drop down. Punch in the tournament you just finished and increment the 'Num Running' second 1 number. This will take all the data from the tournament that was just run and take it off the 'teams entered' and 'edit a league' pages. The data is not erased from the database, just it won't show up because the old data doesn't match the new 'tournament_num_running.'
